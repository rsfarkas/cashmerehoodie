Critical Issues in Thesis

The most critical issue in my thesis is the challenge inherent in executing behavior change in users. Success will require motivating users to take actions that they will likely find tedious or uncomfortable, with no near-term prospect of reward. It will be difficult to find an effective form of extrinsic motivation for users.
