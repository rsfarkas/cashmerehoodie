User Research

Some keywords young adults ages 18-22 connect with are customizable and guidance.1 Young adults are typically seeking inspiration, connection, and convenience.2 Being less commited to traditional institutions, young adults live in the moment and demand greater work life balance. They are comfortable collecting, curating, and sharing data.3

Young adults have different habits then previous generations, with only 50% visiting a doctor's office in the past year as opposed to 75%. 28% admit to self-diagnosing and 36% report trying sel treatment before consulting a doctor. Doctors are sought out less for advice, with almost 25% called on friends or family for medical advice. Around 50% believe that good work life balance is more important to their health and wellness than regular dental or medical exams.  4

c2b solutions breaks the demographic down into different types: 5

  • Self Achievers The most proactive and wellness-oriented. Self Achievers want to take control, but appreciate directive guidance. Willing to spend whatever it takes to be healthy.
  • Balance Seekers Proactive and wellness-oriented, but downplay the role of healthcare professionals. Prefer having options instead of one path to wellness. They prefer suggestions from healthcare professionals instead of orders.
  • Priority Jugglers Less proactive and engaged in personal health. Require higher level of interaction to maintain focus on health.
  • Willful Endurers Very independent and the least proactive about health and wellness. Difficult to motivate toward healthy living.
  • Direction Takers Do not actively engage in their heakth and wellbeing. Reacts only when necessary and follow the prescribed route.

Color Pallete Research

I began the week by doing research on color palletes for medical applications and facilities. White is commonly used as a base for medical applications as it feels professional and calming as a base hue. Blues are associated with positive feelings, while greens symbolize health and prospertity. Darker greens are tiring on the eyes so lighter greens are typically used.3 A balance of warm and cool colors is necessary in order to ensure that medical applications don't feel too cold.2

The color pallette will be key in making the web based application marketable to young adults. Because pink is often associated with maternity, I set out to find some alternatives to millenial pink.3 Some alternatives I sourced were botanical green, violet verbena, cinco de mayo, navy peony, shaded spruce, millenial pink, amber, and pink ground.
