Desk Buddy

Feed your feelings.

February 25th, 2017

My interest in healthcare and education began before I started at DT. My Major Studio 1 projected was an interactive storybook that taught children soft skills, providing them feedback through a stuffed animal and screen prompts. For Major Studio 2 I focused on healthcare, spending the semester researching addiction and creating an application which collected passive data from an e-cigarette. In order to use the group mentality to help addicts quit, mE-Cig created and shared a data visualization with the users 'quitting buddies', and sent buddies alerts when the user was likely to smoke.

Over the summer I began interning at a healthcare imaging and data mangement company. I noticed that there was a huge focus on healthcare when an individual is very sick, but continuing care as well as routine healthcare did not recieve as much attention. I became curious if there was a way to use monitering as a preventative measure, as well as a way to increase people's understanding of their diagnosis in order to prevent hosipital readmittance.

After noticing that many of these problems stemmed from an individuals socioeconomic status, I became interested in the lack of financial literacy that exists among millenial women. These three areas of focus inspired my 9x9 grid.
