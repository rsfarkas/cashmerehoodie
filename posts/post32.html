Preliminary Interviews

Medical needs of young adults.

September 9th, 2017

Interview Summary

Young Adults

Number of Interviewees: 7

Questions asked:

  • What do you do to prevent yourself from becoming sick?
  • What if any, elements do you currently monitor (health, bp, etc)?
  • How often do you wake up feeling slightly sick, or sub optimal?
  • How often do you communicate with your healthcare provider?
  • Would you prefer increased frequency of interaction?

Findings

  • Sleep, exercise, and proper nutrition were the most common methods used to stave off illness.
  • Height and weight were the most common elements monitored. A few participants checked their BP and Cholesterol bi-yearly.
  • Participants mostly felt healthy, but experience minor symptoms a few times a month.
  • Most of the survey participants communicate with their doctor between every 90 days and twice a year.
  • Survey participants were hesitant to have more interactions with their health care providers. They felt that if they were getting added value or if their provider was particularly knowledgable to their specific needs they would be open to it.
