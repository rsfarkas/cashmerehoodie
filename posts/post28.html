Tribe

Find your people.

February 5th, 2017

Design Question

How might we call attention to the dangers and absurdity of xenophobia by revealing the inherent similarities, rather than the differences, in the human experience?

The domains I was interested in exploring were xenophobia and interactivity in the context of environment, both physical and nonphysical.

Design Values

  • Critical of current political state
  • Speculative
  • Manipulates environment

Second Prototype

After the feedback from the first presentation of my prototype, I decided to change the context for the prototype. A disaster was not necessary to get people to use this object if the setting were made more dire.

In order to flesh out ideas about the physical object, I built a physical protoype to test. Using littleBits I connected a battery, a bluetooth low energy bit, and a splitter to connect a vibration motor and led light. I used the bluetooth low energy bit to connect to the littleBits application, where I was able to make the vibration motor vibrate and LED light glo stronger and weaker by using the slider.

After testing the physical prototype, I determined that the vibration of the object was too disruptive. The device's notifications should be gentle, allowing the user to hide them from others if necessary. The glow made users feel comforted and safe, making them more likely to approach strangers who were also glowing.

Third Prototype

The feedback that I received from my second proposal mostly related to my concept. In order to better address xenophobia with a critical approach to current politics, the narrative needed to focus on domineering government control.

I recreated the narrative to make this wearable device mandatory. In this adjusted reality the Global Government has officially codified society into Enclaves.

The product proposal also included a user guide. This guide was intended to appear as a sample packet that a citizen would receive from the Global Government. The guide walks the user through setup, use, and troubleshooting. As the guide is created by the Global Government it markets Tribe in a positive light that helps citizens find their Enclaves.
