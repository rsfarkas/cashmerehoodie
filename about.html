About

Originally from Chicago, I earned my A.A. in fashion design at FIDM in Los Angeles. I then completed my B.A. in International Studies at Fordam University Lincoln Center. While completing my thesis on women's issues in the Middle East, I discovered my drive to utilize technology to make information more accessible.

As a master's candidate at Parson's Design and Technology program, I feel that it was important not only to document my process, but also my experience as a female trying to break into the tech industry.

Like anything else, the tech industry has a dress code. Engineers typically dress casually, with a hoodie being a staple item. The name Cashmere Hoodie is derived from my desire to bring my own perspective to the tech industry.
